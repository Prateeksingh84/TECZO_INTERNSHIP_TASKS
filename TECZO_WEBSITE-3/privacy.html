Privacy Policy

This page explains how Teczo Softwares handles contact form, chatbot and website enquiry information.

Information we collect

We may collect your name, email, company, phone number, service interest, project details and consent status when you submit an enquiry through the website form or chatbot.

How we use information

We use enquiry data to contact you, understand your requirement, provide project estimates, improve service quality and measure website performance.

Analytics and cookies

The website may use analytics events to understand form starts, submissions, CTA clicks and visitor engagement. Non-essential tracking should be configured with consent requirements where applicable.
